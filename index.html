About Hiking Honchos

Hiking Honchos is owned by Wayne Campbell, a former United States Marine with a passion for the outdoors. Currently studying Computer Science at the University of Maryland, Wayne combines his love for nature with his leadership skills to provide unforgettable outdoor experiences.

Our Mission

At Hiking Honchos, we are dedicated to creating exceptional outdoor adventures. Led by Wayne, our team ensures that every trip is safe, enjoyable, and memorable.

University of Maryland Trip Leader

Wayne also serves as a trip leader at the University of Maryland, guiding students in activities such as hiking, canoeing, and caving. His military background enhances the safety and organization of each trip, fostering teamwork and adventure.
