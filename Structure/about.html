About

Hiking Honchos is a travel agency that is focused around adventureous excursions. We are partned with many different supplier so that we are able to give you the journey you've have always dreamt of. We have both custom made and prebuilt trips, ranging from whatever specifications fit your story.
Hiking Honchos is owned by Wayne Campbell, a former United States Marine who is studying Computer Science at the University of Maryland. Wayne found his love for the outdoors from his service and he currently works as a trip leader at the University of Maryland taking students hiking, canoeing, caving and other recreational activites alike.
